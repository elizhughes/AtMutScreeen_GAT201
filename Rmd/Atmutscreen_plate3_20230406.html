At Plate reader assay 3 : Check phenotype of dblGAT201 At mutants in RPMI at 37 degrees

Test WT KN99-alpha and GAT201 deletion mutant 003(Madhani), and At dbl-GAT201 mutant P2.7ZH_A10-A12 and B1-B6 from Plate 1_P2.7ZH(glycerol stock, called dblGAT201a) and At dbl-GAT201 mutant P2.28EH_A10-A12 and B1-B6 from Plate 1_P2.28EH(glycerol stock, called dblGAT201b) in RPMI at 37 degrees. 1 Bioreps, 3 Techreps each. Grow 5ml culture from colony (1 colony = 1 Biorep) in YPD, 30C, 180 rpm ON. Seed at OD 600nm = 0.2 , 200 ul per well. Run for 137 cycles.

Calculate median OD for blank wells for each growth medium RPMI in first hour

blank_OD_summary <- annotated %>%
      filter(is.na(Strain), Time < 1/24) %>%
      group_by(Media) %>%
  summarise(OD_median=median(OD595),
            OD_mean=mean(OD595),
            OD_max=max(OD595),
            OD_min=min(OD595))
print(blank_OD_summary)

Subtract blank OD to make corrected OD and Plot OD_corrected v Time (hrs) RPMI

normalisedOD <- annotated %>%
  left_join(blank_OD_summary, by="Media") %>%
  mutate(OD_corrected = OD595 - OD_median)
