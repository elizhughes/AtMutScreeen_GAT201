At Plate reader assay 2 : Check phenotype of dblGAT201 At mutants in RPMI at 37 degrees

Note: This is a repeat of At mutagenesis Plate reader assay 1. Plate 1 was run by Zhenzhen He and plate 2 was run by Bing Du as training.

Test WT KN99-alpha and GAT201 deletion mutant 003(Madhani), and At dbl-GAT201 mutant P2.7ZH_A1-A9 from Plate 1_P2,7ZH(glycerol stock, called dblGAT201a) and At dbl-GAT201 mutant P2.28EH_A1-A9 from Plate 1_P2.28EH(glycerol stock, called dblGAT201b) in RPMI at 37 degrees. 1 Bioreps, 3 Techreps each. Grow 5ml culture from colony (1 colony = 1 Biorep) in YPD, 30C, 180 rpm ON. Seed at OD 600nm = 0.2 , 200 ul per well. Run for 140 cycles.

Calculate median OD for blank wells for each growth medium RPMI

blank_OD_summary <- annotated %>%
   filter(is.na(Strain), Time < 1/24) %>%
  filter(Well != "H3") %>%
  group_by(Media) %>%
  summarise(OD_median=median(OD595),
            OD_mean=mean(OD595),
            OD_max=max(OD595),
            OD_min=min(OD595))
print(blank_OD_summary)

normalisedOD <- annotated %>%

  mutate(OD_corrected = OD595 - OD_median)
